OBJECTIVES

The University of Ngaoundere Innovation Centre of Excellence (UNICE) has as mission to produce innovation and innovators through three (3) approaches:

EDUCATION

Our multi-disciplinary educational mission seeks to engage faculty and students in close collaboration with corporate partners to develop and commercialise new products and services

COLLABORATION

Bring industry thought leaders together with university experts to seed innovative thinking and enable collaboration in design, technology, prototyping, market studies, products, services and business models.

INCUBATION

Support innovation activities through the use of the Innovation Centre of Excellence facilities, research laboratories, incubators and financial support mainly from the University of Ngaoundere Venture Development Fund (UNVDF).
